PRIVACY POLICY

İzmir Metro Otobüs | Last updated: February 2026

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

This app collects:

  • Location data (only when you use the app, to show nearby stops)
  • Usage analytics (anonymous, to improve the app)
We do NOT collect:
  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Payment information
  • Contacts or messages

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • Location: To show nearby transit stops and calculate routes
  • Analytics: To understand which features are used and improve the app

3. DATA SHARING

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Anonymous analytics data is processed by:

  • Amplitude (analytics)

4. DATA STORAGE

All transit data is stored locally on your device.

We do not store your location history on any server.

5. YOUR RIGHTS

You can:

  • Disable location access in your phone settings
  • Delete the app to remove all local data

6. ADS

This app displays ads via Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization.

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

7. CHILDREN

This app is not directed to children under 13.
